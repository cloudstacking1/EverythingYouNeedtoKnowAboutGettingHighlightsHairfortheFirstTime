What is highlights hair?

Highlights hair is a popular hair coloring technique where specific strands of hair are lightened to create contrast, dimension, and a brighter, more dynamic look. Unlike full hair color, which changes the entire color of your hair, highlights focus on sections usually from root to tipusing shades that are a few levels lighter than your natural hair. This technique enhances the hairs texture, adds movement, and can frame the face beautifully. Whether you're aiming for a subtle sun-kissed glow or a bold, edgy transformation, highlights are a versatile option that can be tailored to suit every hair type and personal style.

Frequently Asked Questions

The longevity of your highlights depends on factors such as hair growth rate and maintenance routine. Typically, highlights can last up to three months. To keep them looking fresh, we recommend scheduling touch-ups every four to eight weeks.
Any lightening process can cause some damage to your hair. However, at Riccardo Maggiore Salon, we use high-quality products and offer treatments like Olaplex to minimize damage. Olaplex helps repair and protect your hair during the coloring process, keeping it strong and healthy.
Traditional highlights use foils to create uniform lightened strands, while balayage involves hand-painting color onto sections of hair for a more natural, sun-kissed look. Balayage typically requires less frequent touch-ups than foiled highlights.
